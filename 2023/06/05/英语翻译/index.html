  • Spring Festival couplets are consist of two verses and horizontal scroll of four words,The verses and horizontal scroll are written on red papers with gold or black ink,red means lucky,gold means fortune
  • Spring Festival couplets are sticked on both sides of the door and the upper of door frame.
  • The verses on Spring Festival couplets represent the characteristics of chinese traditional poem,The two verses have the same number of words and relevant contents
  • horizontal scroll highlights the theme of Spring Festival couplets,makes it even better
  • Spring Festival couplets depict vivid images with simple words and express beautiful wishes.
  • people will realize that the Spring Festival has officially begun,when every famliy sticks Spring Festival couplets.
