1、名言评论

For this part, you are allowed 30 minutes to write an essay commenting on the quote “What is worth doing is worth doing well. “You should write at least 150 words but no more than 200 words.

  • There is a simple but enlightening saying -“What is worth doing is worth doing well”.Then ,what is worth doing? My answer is enjoying the thing you love,taking responsibility and cultivating a habit of lifetime learning.
  • To begin with,enjoying the thing our love may arouse our passion ,bring us motivation to move forward and help us grow up.Thus,Provided that one intends to acqurie and enjoy long-time happiness,it is advisable for us to do what they love and do it well.
  • In addition, it is of great necessity for us,as young people ,to be responsible in life,work,and study.Doing our best to take responsibility,with no doubt,will foster our concrete skills,ensure more useful results,and enable us to go further on the way to success.
  • Meanwhile,the profound meaning in celebrities^,^ life persuaded us that the habit of lifelong learning is an engine which drives both our study and future career. Living in an era of fierce competition,every youngster should be educated to gain new skills and knowledge to adapt to the speedy social development.If one rests,they will rust.
  • As a matter of fact,there are so many other things that are worth doing well.We can make a promising and hopeful future only by doing what is worth dong well.

2、意义阐述

You should write at least 150 words but no more than 200 words.

  • There is a simple but enlightening sentence - “Nowadays more and more people keep learning new skills to adapt to a fast-changing world”,which can be naturally associated with the necessity of acquiring new skills.Provided that we intend to achieve long-term success in the ever-changing world,it is advisable for us to master as many new skills as possible.
  • As a college student, I am convicend that we should equip ourselves with three major skills: foreign language skills,interpersonal communication skills and innovation skills.To begin with, foreign language skill is like a stepping stone which enables us to travel or work freely in world without commuication barriers.
  • Besides,living in an are of globalization,people are closely connected with each other in study ,work and life.Without interpersonal commuication skill,our life and career would be a mess.
  • Meanwhile,the profound meaning in celebrities career persuaded that innovation skill is the engine which drives both our life and future career.
  • In a word,it is of great necessity for us to adapt new skills,we can make a promising and hopeful future only by learning new skills.

3、措施解释

  • Supposing that youngsters intend to acquire and enjoy wonderful and rewarding living,it is advisable for them to have a good balance between job responsibilities and pesonal interests.How to achieve that? There are several measures.
  • To begin with,college students are supposed to improve their working efficiency.**Without a doubt,efficiency is the stepping stone which enhances their speed of working,ensures more useful results,and enables them to go further on the way of both work and life.**Living in an era of intense competition, without efficiency,they are prone to confine themselves to poor abilities and narrow-mindedness.
  • In addition,it is rational for youngsters to limit the number of hobbies,since spending too much time on interests may distract their major tasks.A typical example is that once trapped by a host of hobbies including playing chess,and painting,my friend Dave had no time to finish working.
  • Last but not the least,as a yongster myself,I am deeply convinced that we ought to give our priority to job responsibilities,the rest of time can cultivate personal interests.
  • In conclusion,the above measures will be the keys to balancing the two.
